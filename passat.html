Obituary

2002 Volkswagon Passat

"It drove and then it didn't."

We are gathered here today to bid farewell to the dear Passat. It was a good and loyal vehicle, but on Monday March 5th, 2018, at the VW hospital (Penske) it was pronounced dead. Its primary cause of death was transmission failure, among other previously unknown ailments. It will be missed.
A special thanks to Dad for selling me such a nice car. It was great.
