"Free from Spying Eyes"

Have you ever wished you had more privacy online? Or secretly thought it would be nice if not ALL your emails were scanned for content so that you could have the privilege of receiving targeted adds? Well you've come to the right place! Bronnenmail exists on a server in the cloud that is owned and operated by yours truly. That means that none of your emails will ever be scanned, read, or otherwise: they will remain private. That's not to say that once they leave the server they are not vulnerable to capture by Big Brother, but that's what PGP is for.

Free Email

Here at Bronnenmail we don’t sell your information. We believe the freedom of information is of the upmost importance. That’s why our service is free to all! Just click on the getting started button to set up your very own Bronnenmail email account.

Bronnenmail was set up using Mail-in-a-Box. Mail-in-a-Box lets you become your own mail service provider in a few easy steps. It’s sort of like making your own gmail, but one you control from top to bottom. Technically, Mail-in-a-Box turns a fresh cloud computer into a working mail server. But you don’t need to be a technology expert to set it up. Click here to get your own Mail-in-a-Box up and running.
